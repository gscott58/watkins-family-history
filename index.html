Watkins Family History


Christine. . .


I put together the “Watkins Family History” web page as a tribute to your mother.  My intent was to tell how she met your father and how they journeyed with four children from the Carolinas to Chicago, Illinois.

And to tell how upon arriving in Chicago, you' all family grew.  I also wanted to provide an opportunity for each of your sisters and brothers to write their own "life story".

Like my mother, your mother has passed away, leaving her life and times story for your family to reminisce. No darkness can be spoken of the tribute I put together for your mother.

It is the life and times of your mother that lives on in you. Like how you always speak of your mother sitting at the head of her table with a dish rag in hand, ready to feed her family and wipe up any spills.

It's sad your sisters and brothers will be deprived of your mother's life and times story because you put a shroud of darkness over the tribute I put together for her.

Despite how you feel, your mother's spirit lives on in each of you anyway. . .

Gregory Scott Sr.
